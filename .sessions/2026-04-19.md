## What Was Built

### Phase 4 — Particle Physics
- **`js/physics/particle-physics.js`** — Implemented from stub. `ParticlePhysics(engine)` class. `step()` runs Velocity Verlet with 50 sub-steps/frame. Anti-tunneling: per sub-step, checks `distanceToSegment(prev, curr) * PX_TO_M < R_CAPTURE_M` against every charge, snaps to `closestPointOnSegment` on capture. Energy sampled every 3 sub-steps into `particle.trail`. Trail trimmed to `MAX_TRAIL_SECONDS` (sim-time). Exit at ±50 px margin sets `state='exited'`.
- **`js/renderer/particle-renderer.js`** — Implemented from stub. `drawTrail` (age-faded alpha per segment; segments past `scrubTimestamp` dimmed ×0.2), `drawParticle` (r=8, glow, white ring when selected, `+/−` glyph), `drawForceVector` (log-scaled, `#ff8844`, label "F⃗"), `drawVelocityVector` (log-scaled against `maxSpeed`, `#44ff88`, label "v⃗").
- **`js/main.js`** — Instantiated `FieldEngine` + `ParticlePhysics`. rAF loop steps all `state='moving'` particles (pause-aware); tracks `state.maxSpeed`. Drag start → `state='held'`, v=0, trail cleared; release → `drop_*` updated, `state='moving'`. Line preset expanded to 10 charges at 120 px spacing.
- **`js/renderer/canvas-manager.js`** — Wired `drawTrail`/`drawParticle`/`drawForceVector`/`drawVelocityVector` into render steps 4 & 6. `render()` now accepts `maxSpeed`.
